What are LabelHolders?

A common design trend in forms is to use placeholder text in place of labels. This creates a clean looking form, but causes a few issues. Semantically it's a problem for accessability. It's also a problem when users have entered info or a form has been prepopulated there's nothing to identify what the field is for.

LabelHolders takes semantically marked up labels and positions them inside their inputs to look like placeholders. The labels can then be moved and resized around the input so they are still visible once users have entered data.

LabelHolders will automatically move to the "active" position when actual placeholder text is present as shown in the Phone number field in the demo below.
